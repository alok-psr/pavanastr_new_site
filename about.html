THE PAVANASTR STORY

Engineered for India

2019

Pavanastr Technologies founded by a consortium of aerospace and defence engineers with a mission to develop indigenous UAV systems for India's strategic requirements.

2020

First prototype of GANDIV-I successfully tested in controlled environments. Established partnerships with leading defence institutions and DRDO.

2021

GANDIV-I completed high-altitude trials in Ladakh at 5,400m ASL. Successfully demonstrated autonomous mission capabilities and AI-assisted targeting systems.

2022

GANDIV-II and GANDIV-III combat systems underwent field validation. Secured strategic partnerships with defence procurement agencies and state-owned enterprises.

2023

Expanded product line with Nano Drone swarm platforms. Conducted maritime surveillance operations along India's coastal borders with naval partners.

2024

GANDIV-IV counter-UAS system operationalized. Completed 200+ successful field deployments. Established R&D centers for next-generation autonomous platforms.
